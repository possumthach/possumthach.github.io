ABOUT

My name is Casper. I am a multidisciplinary artist, which means I do a lot of different things with varying levels of amateurishness. You can see some of it by clicking the PROJECTS link. Currently, my main focus is IMMORTAL HAND, IMMORTAL EYE, an audiotextual hypernovel about exploring and exploiting the nature of reality.

I make visual art primarily in Krita, with occasional assists from GIMP (mostly for gifs). I use DesignDoll for pose referencing. I make music in Reaper with Audacity for some additional editing; previously I used Cakewalk (2023-2025), Bandlab (2023), Soundtrap (2019-2022), and Soundation (<2019). I write in the notes app on my phone. I made this website in Visual Studio Code.

You can email me at casperfelicien@gmail.com or contact me through any of the sites linked in the OTHER PLACES tab (tumblr is most reliable.)
