ABOUT ME

My name is Casper. I make art of various kinds. You can see some of it by clicking the PROJECTS link above. I'm nonbinary and use he/they pronouns (as in, either "he" or "they" is fine, you don't need to vacillate every sentence.) More personal information about me:

  • Zodiac sign: Pisces
  • Blood type: O+
  • Known allergens: cashews
  • Favorite Mars rover: Sojourner

I make visual art primarily in Krita, with occasional assists from GIMP (mostly for gifs). I use DesignDoll for pose referencing. As of 2024, I make music exclusively in Cakewalk with Audacity for some additional editing; previously I used Bandlab (2023), Soundtrap (2019-2022), and Soundation (<2019). I write in the notes app on my phone. I made this website in Visual Studio Code.

You can email me at casperfelicien@gmail.com or contact me through any of the sites linked in the OTHER PLACES tab (tumblr is most reliable.)
