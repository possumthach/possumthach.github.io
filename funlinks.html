FUN LINKS

This section is just a collection of online stuff I think is cool. Someday I might get around to writing "reviews" of some of these things, which will get put on their own "recommendations" page. "Maybe."

READABLE THINGS
(including readable things with pictures)

Cleveland Quixotic

isekai with some of the best character writing and setting design i've ever seen. better than worth the candle and one-fifth the length.
A Beginner's Guide to the End of the Universe

suggestion-powered webcomic originally posted to the mspaintadventures forums in 2010. guaranteed to contain less than 2% homestuck by weight.
Mother Horse Eyes

reddit's favorite anonymously guerilla-published nonlinear danielewski-vania. about a lot of things, including alien crab romance.
Has anyone heard of the Left/Right Game?

r/nosleep story that manages to outperform others in its genre by cleverly subverting the asinine plausibility rules and telling an actually compelling narrative.
17776

text-and-video story about sports in post-mortality america, starring three sapient space probes. punches (emotionally) above its weight class and wins every time.
GODHEAD

wonderfully stylized and experimental webcomic pushing the boundaries of browser-based art. has music, animation, interactive pages, hovertext narration, 3D screens, et cetera. we're not in 2009 anymore, dorothy.
The Northern Caves

unique story about all-consuming obsession, pattern-seeking, and filling in the gaps. not as bombastic as some, but of the type to stick in your brain for months after reading.
Kij Johnson

technically an entire author is not a "readable thing", but you can certainly read all these short stories linked on this page. imaginative and thought-provoking across the gamut of scifi, magical realism, and fantasy.

PLAYABLE THINGS

Heisei Pistol Show

punchy neon-coated rpgmaker game that manages to be both absurdly hilarious and emotionally sincere. RM2k jank adds to the charm.
With Those We Love Alive

bridging the gap between "readable thing" and "playable thing", an interactive story set in a dreamlike and macabre world. frankly ahead of its time.
Our Dirge

(currently demo) dialogue-centered VNRPG set in a painterly liminal space. personable, precise in scope, and naturally engaging.

WATCHABLE THINGS

Petscop

video series constantly being misinterpreted by "horror youtubers". captivating, meticulously crafted, represents the distilled and bottled aspirations of every video game creepypasta from the past decade.
After Last Season

extraordinarily bad movie from 2009 that fails in literally every aspect. genuinely difficult to watch. holds a special place in my heart and brain for its weird barren sets, clumsy CGI sequences, and incoherent sound mixing.
