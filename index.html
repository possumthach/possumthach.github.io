WELCOME TO MY WEB SITE...


...the personal page of CASPER FELICIEN. This site is perpetually under construction; some parts are more under construction than others. Have a look around and find out which ones!

Note that this site is coded by an amateur and intended to be viewed on a desktop or laptop computer using Firefox. It may break at small resolutions or on touchscreen monitors. If you're on a mobile device, try turning on reader mode for better accessibility. I make no concessions for those using a non-Mozilla browser, though.

Some pages on this site make use of hovernotes, indicated by a dashed underline. They contain additional, non-essential commentary in the title field, which should appear if you hover your mouse over them.
